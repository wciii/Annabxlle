Band & Friends

Meet the talented people behind the music

Annabelle's Band

Nigel Acard

Lead Guitar

If your wildest dream is seeing The Ghost of Duane Allman / Stevie Ray / Jimi Hendrix shredding Chappell Roan, Nigel is your dream. Wait til you see his signature pose.

Louis Aurisy

Drums, Backing Vocals

Louis's energy on the drum kit is unmatched. He brings his all every performance, even to the brink of passing out if need be. If you're lucky you might get to see him sing Teenage Dirtbag, baby.

David Brickman

Keys, Train Whistle, Bass

Dave loves music almost as much as he loves trains. Counting King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard as his top inspiration, Dave is the X, Y, and Z Factor. Catch him running the Pawling High School Track Team, if you can.

Arlan Thornquist

Bass, Vocals, Guitar, Keys

The bands resident tattoo artist and king of all trades in the music world, Arlan does it all. He wrote his first song Boom Chicka Pop when he was 7, and honestly it holds up.

Friends & Collaborators

Adri Rogowsky

Backing Vocals, Bass

Passionate fan and occasional backing vocalist/bassist contributor, Adri has not missed a single Annabelle show, nor will she.

Jillian

Lyrics, Annabelle's Sister

Jill (Bird) is the best. She's a talented lyricist that has assisted Annabelle's writing process numerous times.

Robin

Electric Violin

Did you know electric violin was a thing? Neither did we but we're sure glad Robin showed us because it's awesome. On top of fronting her own band, teaching yoga classes, and skateboarding the Hudson Valley, she still finds the time to jam and perform with Annabelle.

Will

Manager?

Yeah Will doesn't really know what he does either, but he feels like he's doing it well, and that's what counts. He did make this website though which he thinks is pretty cool.
