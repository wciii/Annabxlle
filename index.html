Biography

Annabelle is an emerging artist and songwriter based in NYC and Nashville who has been making waves with her relatable content and original music. Her journey from upstate New York to collaborating with Nashville's Songhouse has shaped her songwriting skills and stage presence.

Influenced by a diverse range of female artists including Taylor Swift, Olivia Rodrigo, and FLETCHER, Annabelle has been steadily releasing music since February 2024. Her debut single "Comparison" launched her career with a catchy anthem about intrusive thoughts and self-comparison. This was followed by her most streamed single "Traumablind" in April, showcasing her artistic growth.

Newest Release: "Psycho"

Annabelle's latest single, "Psycho," is a catchy, relatable, funny, and quirky tune that tackles the challenges of new relationships burdened by past toxic relationships. The song celebrates the resilience of love and the decision to move forward together.
